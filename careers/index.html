Software Engineer — Entrepreneurial Material


Join our core team and work on cutting-edge technologies that have the potential to change the future of the internet. Our novel and unique approach gives you insight into an amazing new field, that you can not get anywhere else. Combined with an entrpreneurial environment and endless opportunities we offer, you get a position that is far more than a job.

What we expect from you?

Experience with blockchain technology (e.g. ethereum, hyperledger, bitcoin) is favorable, but not necessary. You should have good knowledge in major computer science fields, most notably distributed systems and algorithms, and software engineering and be able to learn or deepen your skills as we move forward.

  • A good understanding about distributed architectures
  • Experience in web development, micro services and object oriented programming (e.g. NodeJS, Javascript, CSS, HTML, RESTFul API)
  • Experience with NoSQL databases
  • At least a basic understanding of applied cryptographic principles (e.g. application of asymmetric and symmetric encryption, signatures and hashing)

You should be curious and have a strong desire to learn and growth.


What you get?

As part of the engineering team you will start with the development of Proof of Concepts and Pilots with major global financial institutions. By closely working with our business team and tight involvement with customers you will help to bring these first versions of the product from early stage to production. On the way you will have the opportunity to deepen your understanding of a very hot field and growth with the company.
