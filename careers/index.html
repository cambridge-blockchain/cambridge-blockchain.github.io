Positions

Software Engineer — Entrepreneurial Material

We are looking for a software engineer to join our core team to help develop our fundamental technology. You will be working with cutting-edge blockchain technologies with an initial focus on contributing on both the front- and back-end of a typical software stack. Depending on your familiarity with blockchain systems you will have the opportunity to move deeper into blockchain application as you master our technology and prove your blockchain skills.

We seek sharp and curious minds with...

  • 1-3 years software engineering experience developing back-end and front-end logic for production systems
  • a love for working in and collaborating across teams
  • web and backend development expertise (for full stack web and distributed systems), including NodeJS, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
  • a strong understanding of
    • web principles, such as RESTFul APIs, asynchronous real-time communication
    • distributed systems architectures and principles
    • object oriented programming style
  • the ability to apply previously learned programming skills to new programming language and environments
  • and a basic understanding of applied cryptographic principles, (e.g. asymmetric and symmetric encryption, signatures, hashing, authentication)

Our ideal candidate would also have...

  • a deeper understanding of distributed systems and algorithms
  • familiarity with alternative database models, especially NoSQL types
  • a strong understanding of cryptographic systems and algorithm
  • experience with biometric systems for authentication and identification purposes
  • formal training in computer science, math, physics or engineering
  • prior experience in an entrepreneurial environment

You may bring some, but not all of these things. That's OK - we know what it takes to build companies - a strong work ethic, a keen mind and a love to make things happen. If you are curious and bright, eager to learn and grow, and love to build things, we still want to talk to you!
